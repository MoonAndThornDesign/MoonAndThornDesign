Privacy Policy

Moon and Thorn Designs — Last updated: June 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Moon and Thorn Designs Pinterest integration tool, a personal application used solely by the owner of Moon and Thorn Designs to post digital artwork to Pinterest.

Information collected

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties. The only data used is the owner's Pinterest access token, which is entered directly by the owner and is not transmitted to or stored by any third-party service other than Pinterest.

How information is used

The Pinterest access token is used exclusively to authenticate with the Pinterest API in order to create pins on the owner's own Pinterest boards. No user data is collected, processed, or sold.

Third-party services

This application interacts with the Pinterest API. Pinterest's own privacy policy applies to all data processed by Pinterest. Please refer to Pinterest's Privacy Policy for more information.

Data sharing

No personal data is shared with any third parties. This application is for personal use only and does not serve any external users.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact Moon and Thorn Designs via the Etsy shop at moonandthorndesigns.etsy.com.
